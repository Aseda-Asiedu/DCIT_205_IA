Our Facilities

The school facility is much more than a passive container of the educational process: it is, rather, an integral component of the conditions of learning. Our Facilities are top-notch and are constructed with well materials, with students in mind

Hygiene Environs

The University of Ghana is committed to providing a holistic educational experience, emphasizing not only academic excellence but also the well-being of its students, staff, and faculty.

Ultra Modern Laboratory

The computer labs at the University of Ghana are equipped with cutting-edge infrastructure, featuring the latest hardware and software technologies. High-performance computers, modern servers, and advanced networking capabilities create an environment conducive to exploring the frontiers of technology.

World Class library

The commitment to technology extends beyond the computer labs to the library, ensuring that students have access to the latest research tools. The university invests in updated library management software, electronic databases, and high-speed internet connectivity, facilitating efficient research and exploration of academic resources.

Courses We Offer

We offer a wide range of premium courses. These courses are up to date and visionarily defined as per the institutions standard.

Undergraduate Programmes

The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

MPhil and MSC Programmes

The Department of Computer Science University of Ghana currently runs Masters of Science (MSc.) in Computer Science and Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science. The MSc. is a 1-year (weekend/evening) programme with a capstone project while the MPhil is a 2-year (full-time) programme

PhD Programmes

Our doctoral programme is a full-time research programme with expected duration of 4-years. For the successful completion of this programme, a high level of commitment is required.
