ALUMNI

ALUMNI OF GREATNESS

"Students yesterday, alumni for a lifetime." Your University journey hardly ends when you graduate from here; it's actually just begun. Our Alumni are our biggest champions and the work they continue to do after graduating from this whole institution helps extend our impact. Across the world, and particularly on the African continent, our over 1000 alumni work in a variety of ways to make impact. As an Alumni of such a prestige institution one can gain access to a network of graduates and classmates who can provide valuable support and connections.
